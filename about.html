About Me

Hello, I’m @MyWeb3Alias — a creative technologist, NFT collector, and blockchain enthusiast based in Melbourne, Australia.

I created this space to document my journey through the Web3 universe, share thoughts on decentralization, showcase my NFT collection, and write about the communities and tech shaping the future of the internet.

Outside of the metaverse, I’m into gaming, miniatures, sci-fi, and world-building. You'll find that reflected in my blog and the kinds of projects I support.

If you’re interested in collaboration, donations, or just want to say hi — feel free to reach out through my contact page or socials!
